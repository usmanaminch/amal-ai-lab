Day 13 Reflection

Medical ideas you learned

  • Bones are living tissue with layers, blood supply, marrow, and repair cells.
  • X-rays help doctors see the fracture pattern before choosing support.
  • Osteoblasts build bone, while osteoclasts remodel bone.
  • Bone healing has stages: inflammation, soft callus, hard callus, and remodeling.
