Today’s Mission

Click the real body parts

Click the heart to start blood pumping. Click the lungs to start breathing. You can turn on more than one body part at the same time.

How to use it: Click the actual heart, lungs, brain, bones, muscles, skin, teeth, or jaw in the diagram. The last thing you click updates the info panel, and multiple body parts can stay active together.

Day 4 Reflection

What this upgrade teaches

  • A diagram can be more interactive when the real parts are clickable.
  • JavaScript can track more than one active body system at a time.
  • Animations can help explain how the body works, not just decorate the page.
