Day 15 Reflection

Medical ideas you learned

  • Muscles contract and pull on bones.
  • Tendons connect muscle to bone.
  • Ligaments connect bone to bone.
  • Tendon healing depends on collagen repair and remodeling.
  • Rehab is usually gradual because healing tissue needs time to get stronger.
