What are patient vitals?

Vitals are body signals that help trained medical teams understand how a patient is doing. This project explains the big ideas in a safe, kid-friendly way.

❤️

Heart Rate

How many times the heart beats in one minute.

🫁

Oxygen

How much oxygen is being carried in the blood.

🌬️

Breathing

How often the patient breathes in one minute.

🌡️

Temperature

How warm the body is.

Memory line: Vitals are clues. The care team watches the clues to help keep the patient safe.

Teamwork

Who watches vitals?

In real care, trained professionals work together and follow safety rules.

🩺

Doctor / Surgeon

Focuses on the medical plan and patient safety.

😷

Anesthesia Team

Specially trained professionals who watch breathing, comfort, and safety.

👩‍⚕️

Nurse

Helps prepare, observe, communicate, and support safe care.

🖥️

Monitor

Shows readings that help the team notice changes.

Safety idea: No one person watches everything alone. Patient safety is teamwork.
