Day 11 Reflection

What this project teaches

  • Plaque removal and brushing are different steps in this model.
  • Toothpaste flavor is a design choice that makes the simulator more fun.
  • Braces use brackets, wires, and color bands to guide teeth over time.
  • The jaw helps chew food by moving food between upper and lower teeth.
