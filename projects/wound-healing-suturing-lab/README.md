# Wound Healing and Suturing Lab · Day 14

A deeper biology and future-surgeon learning project.

## Topics
- Skin layers: epidermis, dermis, hypodermis
- Platelets and clotting
- Fibroblasts and collagen
- Wound types and clinical thinking
- Clean technique and infection prevention
- Simplified suturing and bandaging simulation
- Wound healing stages

## Safety
Educational simulation only. Not medical advice. Real wounds need trained medical professionals.
