Day 14 Reflection

Medical ideas you learned

  • Skin has layers: epidermis, dermis, and hypodermis.
  • Platelets help stop bleeding by forming a clot.
  • Fibroblasts make collagen to strengthen repair tissue.
  • Clean technique matters because germs can cause infection.
  • Sutures help hold wound edges together in this simplified model.
