What is medical imaging?

Medical imaging helps trained doctors and medical teams see inside the body. Different scans show different clues, like bones, soft tissues, organs, or movement.

🦴

X-Ray

Great for seeing bones, fractures, teeth, and chest clues.

🧠

MRI

Great for soft tissues like the brain, muscles, joints, and spine.

🌀

CT Scan

Uses many X-ray pictures to make detailed slice views.

👶

Ultrasound

Uses sound waves to see organs, blood flow, and babies before birth.

Memory line: Each scan has a superpower. Doctors pick the scan that best answers the question.
