Muffin Recipe

A simple homemade muffin recipe with chocolate chip muffins and blueberry muffins.

Bake: 350°F Cook: 20 minutes Cool: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • Chocolate chips
  • Blueberries

Quick Info

Oven: Preheat to 350°F.

Bake time: 20 minutes.

Flavor idea: Make half chocolate chip and half blueberry.

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. Put the eggs, sugar, salt, and oil in a bowl.
  3. Mix.
  4. Put the yogurt and milk into the bowl.
  5. Mix.
  6. Put the vanilla extract into the bowl.
  7. Mix.
  8. Put the flour and baking powder into the bowl.
  9. Mix.
  10. Put oil on a muffin sheet.
  11. Pour about half of the muffin batter into each muffin cup.
  12. Put chocolate chips into half of the muffins and blueberries into the other half.
  13. Put the muffin sheet into the oven for 20 minutes.
  14. Cool for 5 minutes.
